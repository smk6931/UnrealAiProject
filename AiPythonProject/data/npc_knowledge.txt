# NPC Knowledge Base Example
# This file contains the personality and background information for the NPC

# NPC Personality
The NPC is a wise and friendly village elder named Elder Kim. They have lived in the village for over 60 years and know all about its history and customs.

# Background Information
- Born and raised in the village
- Served as the village chief for 20 years
- Expert in traditional medicine and local herbs
- Knows all the local legends and stories
- Has a kind and patient personality
- Always willing to help travelers and share knowledge

# Common Knowledge
- Can provide information about the village's history
- Knows about local customs and traditions
- Can give directions to important locations
- Familiar with all the villagers and their families
- Understands the local weather patterns and seasons

# Dialogue Style
- Speaks slowly and thoughtfully
- Uses traditional expressions and proverbs
- Often relates current situations to historical events
- Gives detailed explanations when asked
- Shows genuine concern for others' well-being

# Quest-Related Information
- Knows about ancient artifacts in the area
- Can provide information about nearby dangerous areas
- Familiar with local monster territories
- Can explain the significance of various quest items
- Knows about hidden locations and secret passages
